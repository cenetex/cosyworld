# The Lantern Keeper

A short, shared fifth-edition-inspired CosyWorld campaign for level-one
characters. The beacon above Mothwood has failed, its keeper Rowan Vale is
missing, and the old road is filling with things that prefer darkness.

The campaign is licensed under CC-BY-4.0 and carries its required SRD 5.1
attribution in `ATTRIBUTION.md` and the compiled world attribution index.

The campaign is designed for one compact arc:

1. Meet Mara Wick at the Wayside Lantern Inn.
2. Follow the extinguished lamps through Mothwood.
3. Recover the keeper's key and learn why Rowan sealed the tower.
4. Cross the Flooded Barrow and confront what is wearing the keeper's shadow.
5. Rekindle the beacon or let the dark claim the road.

Character creation is owned by this pack through `character_creation.json`.
Each archetype uses existing CosyWorld mechanics: an initial Calling, title,
entry room, and one trained knack. SRD classes and spell slots are not implied.

Campaign residents use `ambient_autonomy: false`: they react to player turns and
direct interaction but do not wander or resolve the authored mystery off-screen.
