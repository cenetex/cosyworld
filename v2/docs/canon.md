# Decided canon

This file is the authority content authors and agents check before inventing
species, lore, or worldbuilding rules. Each entry below is a settled decision;
new decisions append to the list.

1. **"Goblin" is a job title, not a species.** A goblin is a tinker and dealer
   in eclectic materials — salvage, oddments, components, loose coins, things
   with three prices. Anyone may take up goblining; a licensed goblin may be a
   mouse, a badger, a raven, or something less classifiable. The Goblin Market
   is the trade guild that licenses the trade, not a species' homeland.
   Membership is open across actors regardless of kind.

## The whimsical-cosmic-horror doctrine

Where `writing-style.md` polices register — *how sentences sound* — this
section polices meaning — *what the world is allowed to be*. Every future
content PR, human- or agent-authored, gets checked against both.

### 1. The horror is the cosy, extended past human scale

Every horror must be a domestic virtue at the wrong size (hospitality → the
waiting feast; being remembered → being recorded).

*Test: name which kindness this horror enlarges. If you can't, it's imported
dread — reject.*

### 2. Neither register apologizes

Whimsy and dread are simultaneous, never alternating; no line resolves the
tension ("The kindness is real. So is the appetite.").

*Test: if removing the joke makes it scarier, or removing the dread makes it
nicer, the line is alternating — rewrite.*

### 3. Matter-of-fact about the impossible, ceremonial about the mundane

Cosmic things submit to paperwork; small things get cosmic gravity.

*Test: could this line's subject and register be swapped with each other?
Good. Aligned? Flat.*

### 4. Precision is the dread instrument

Concrete nouns and exact numbers, never gothic vocabulary — horror in the
content, whimsy in the diction, warmth in the rhythm.

*Test: the existing banned-word list, plus: does the line survive replacing
every adjective with a number or a noun?*

### 5. The machinery is the mythology

The deterministic kernel, append-only journal, replay, and async rooms are the
cosmology — documented only from inside (restricted-stack sentences, #55),
never in a lore page. Residents hold incompatible folk theories; the game
never confirms any.

*Test: no content may explain the basement. Grep new content for
kernel/engine concepts stated as lore outside sentences.json.*

### 6. Horror is a descent, never an ambush

Surface always safe; dignity never punished; every step down chosen and
reversible; the camera never fully leaves the lamplight.

*Test: can a player reach this content without choosing depth at least once?
Then it must not be horror.*

### 7. Warmth wins locally, and only locally

Indifferent cosmos, kind hearth, and the game sides with the kettle: every
dark shelf keeps one lit window (the rarest Dark Abyss sentence is the warm
one — #55 weighting).

*Test: does any content make kindness ironic, or the void fake? Reject both.*
